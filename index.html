The Science

Your voice is data.
Not just expression.

Voice analysis is used in research because speech changes under stress, low mood, neurological load, and emotional suppression.

You can choose your words. Your voice still shows the pattern.

Voice changes appear 5–10 years before motor symptoms in Parkinson's. Bipolar episodes are detectable 48–72 hours before mood shifts. Depression markers are consistent across cultures. The voice does not perform for the analysis.

Stress detection via acoustic features reaches 85–90% accuracy (Hansen & Patil, 2007). Emotion recognition across 104 studies shows 60–65% accuracy from pitch alone (Juslin & Laukka, 2003). Pitch variability decreases 35–45% in depressed patients. Cortisol levels correlate with voice features at r=0.74. The voice predicted burnout three months before self-report.
